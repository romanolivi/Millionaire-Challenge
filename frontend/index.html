Welcome to The Millionaire Challenge!


The instructions for this game are simple, you have 10 fill in the blank questions at a time.

You will earn $100,000 for each correct answer, but getting a question wrong will result in losing your entire balance and finsihing with a balance of $0.

You can choose to end the game once you are satisfied with your balance, or you can go all in to get the Million Dollar Score
